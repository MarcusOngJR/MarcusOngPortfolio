Overview

Developed a desktop productivity software MVP to help users manage tasks, notes, and schedules efficiently. Integrated features such as task prioritization, note-taking with rich text formatting, and calendar synchronization — plus an onboard AI assistant to keep users company during long, isolating work-from-home sessions.

  • Role: Sole developer (design → build → testing)
  • Focus: Reducing task overload + improving daily clarity
  • Core Modules: Tasks, Notes, Calendar, AI assistant
MVP walkthrough — task management, notes, and assistant interactions.

Highlights

  • Task prioritisation + quick reordering for “what to do next”.
  • Rich text notes for meeting capture and planning.
  • Calendar sync to visualise workload across the week.
  • AI assistant to nudge, encourage, and surface reminders.
